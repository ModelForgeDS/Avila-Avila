About Us

Avila & Avila began as a family-owned engineering business in Bogotá, Colombia, founded on hard work, technical expertise and a commitment to quality.

Over the years, the company expanded beyond traditional electrical services and became a multidisciplinary engineering provider delivering industrial installations, precision laser metal fabrication, structural manufacturing and engineering consulting.

Today, we combine family values with modern technology to provide innovative engineering solutions for industrial, commercial and infrastructure projects across Colombia.
